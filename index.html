Bio

Qadree Trimble is a budding entrepreneur who started his first business as a Life Coach and Catering Chef in February of 2020. He decided to learn to code to build his own website and apps in regard to his business. Qadree wants the experience of working at a shop in a tech company so that he can get better at writing code.

His beginnings in the tech world started after he joined the military upon graduating high school. Qadree chose the U.S. Navy and went to school after bootcamp to become an IT. His first station of duty was on the USS Blue Ridge. There he was introduced to LAN Networking. During this time in his career he became a System Administrator and traveled with the Navy to many countries around the world. Qadree finished his service in Washington D.C. and eventually moved to Seattle.
